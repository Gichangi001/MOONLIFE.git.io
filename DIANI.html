DIANI

Diani Beach is a beautiful coastal area located south of Mombasa in Kenya. It is a popular tourist destination due to its white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a wide range of attractions. Here are some of the top attractions to explore in Diani:..

Diani Beach: This stunning beach is the main attraction in Diani. It is known for its soft, powdery sand and turquoise waters. Visitors can relax on the beach, swim, snorkel, or take part in water sports activities.

Shimba Hills National Reserve: This reserve is located just a short drive from Diani and is home to a variety of wildlife, including elephants, giraffes, and antelopes. Visitors can go on a safari, take a nature walk, or visit the stunning Sheldrick Falls.

Kaya Kinondo Sacred Forest: This ancient forest is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to the Digo people. Visitors can take a guided tour of the forest to learn about the local culture and history.

Colobus Conservation: This conservation center is dedicated to the protection of the colobus monkey. Visitors can learn about the conservation efforts and even adopt a monkey.

Mombasa Old Town: This historic town is located just a short drive from Diani and is known for its Arabic and Portuguese architecture. Visitors can explore the narrow streets, visit the Fort Jesus Museum, and shop for local crafts.

Diani Beach Art Gallery: This art gallery showcases the work of local artists and is a great place to buy souvenirs. Skydive Diani: For the adrenaline junkies, Skydive Diani offers an unforgettable experience. Visitors can jump out of a plane and take in the stunning views of Diani from above. Overall, Diani is a great destination for beach lovers, nature enthusiasts, and those interested in history and culture.
